### 🗣️ Natural Language Control
Control robots by **talking to them**:
```
You: "Go to the kitchen carefully"

```

### 🛡️ Adaptive Safety
Watch safety **adapt to context**:
```
"Check battery"        → Low safety    → No confirmation needed
"Go to kitchen"        → Medium safety → Confirmation required
"Emergency: Go NOW!"   → Critical      → Immediate execution
```
