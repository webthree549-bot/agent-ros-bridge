## Concept

A greenhouse where every plant has a voice. Moisture, light, and temperature sensors feed into an AI poet that translates plant states into beautiful, emotional language. Robots respond by tending to the plants' needs.

**The vision:** Plants aren't silent - we just haven't been listening properly.
